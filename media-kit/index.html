{% t media-kit.guidelines-section %}

{% t media-kit.guidelines-description %}

Do
  • Use the official logos from this media kit
  • Maintain the logo's aspect ratio when resizing
  • Ensure adequate contrast between logo and background
  • Use light text logos on dark backgrounds and vice versa
Don't
  • Modify the colors or add effects to the logo
  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Use the logo to imply endorsement without permission
  • Place colored logos on low-contrast backgrounds
